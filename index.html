Welcome to My Underwater World!

Hello world! I'm Ila, a 12-year-old girl with big dreams and creative ideas. This is my space to share all the cool projects I'm working on, games I love to play, and adventures I go on with my friends. I'm passionate about oceans, their creatures, and making the world a better place!

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me drawing, reading about sea creatures, or playing with my friends. I believe that being creative and curious can lead to amazing discoveries!
